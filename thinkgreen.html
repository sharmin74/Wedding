Think GREEN

Committed to Excellence

We at Sweven Events think about environment, due to the on going climate change and turbulences on Mama earth; we gave a thought of eco-friendly events. Planning an environmentally friendly event isn’t any different from other events; its just a change in mindset and choices. Having a less wasteful event doesn’t mean you have to sacrifice your style, and it doesn’t need to take any extra ‘GREEN’ either. SWEVEN EVENTS can provide you with sustainable and chic ways to green any special occasion. We are professionally and personally committed to minimize the negative impact on the environment. A few of our earth friendly practices includes Recycling, Composting, supporting local and eco-friendly business in and around India.
